King Vultman has resurrected himself after being dead for 3200 years. He fully resurrected himself and his deceased wife Queen Victoria. He also resurrected his army of 10,000 Dead Warriors in a mission to end the world!

The Dead Army Club (DAC) is a collection of 1 King, 1 Queen, and 10,000 unique Dead Army Warriors (DAWs).
